Who we are

BUILT FROM
THE GROUND UP

LayerBru is a South African 3D printing business run by two brothers who believe great products shouldn't require a factory or a minimum order of a thousand units.

We design and print everything in-house on professional-grade equipment, giving us full control over quality, colour, and material — from first prototype to final product.

Whether you need something off our catalogue or a completely custom design, we're set up to make it happen quickly and at a price that makes sense for the South African market.
